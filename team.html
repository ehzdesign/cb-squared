“CB Squared (CB2) believes a hair studio grows on satisfied customers. We understand persons hair is very important, that’s why building a strong trust worthy relationship goes a long way in maintaining customer satisfaction. Our attention to detail, product knowledge and professionalism allows new clients to feel confident once they sit in our chair. CB2 Hair Studio is driven on passion, a passion for making people look and feel beautiful. We promote creativity and that each stylist / Barber is up to date with current social trends.
